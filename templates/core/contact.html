Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I become a mentor?

To become a mentor, please fill out our mentor application form on the mentors page. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 3-5 business days.

Is there a cost to ask questions to mentors?

Basic questions are free for all users. For more in-depth consultations or specialized advice, some mentors may offer premium services at reasonable rates.

How long does it take to get a response?

Most mentors respond within 24-48 hours. If you haven't received a response after 3 days, please contact our support team for assistance.
